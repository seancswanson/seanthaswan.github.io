Slow Fashion

The fasion industry is contributing to today's sustainability challenge in a number of ways. It currently uses a flow of natural resources and relies heavily on man made compounds such as synthetic fibers and pesticides. Slow fashion is a movement that focuses on slowing down the fashion industry.

Plastic Reduction

Every piece of plastic that has been produced still exists today. 1/3 of the plastic produced are single use items, sent straight to the landfill. It is polluting our oceans and killing wildlife. Learn what you can do in your everyday life to reduce your impact!
