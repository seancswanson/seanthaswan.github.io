Start your corporate career as a Mailroom Clerk! Try to climb the ranks as you encounter scenarios and events that could skyrocket you to your Golden Throne as Chairman of the Board or be derailed and spend eternity in that god-forsaken letter hell!

Earn confidence points for proper reactions to each week's scenario and if your manager especially approves of your decision you may earn brownie points! You may try for a promotion at any time and can use confidence to increase your chances or if you have enough brownie points you may be given special priveledges! If you don't have enough points then you may relive the past weeks and earn more points.
