About

The abstract: Basically I'm a pretty cool human who loves to breakdance, paint watercolor landscapes, go on long walks, read eastern philosophy(or anything fantasy/sci-fi), and it so happens that I'm also a web developer!

If you'd like the full story, click here!

I grew up in the beautiful pacific northwest where the rain falls aplenty and single-origin cold brew is the preferred stimulant.

My most recent adventure was a 3-month immersive (and quite intensive) web development bootcamp through General Assembly. overall the curriculum included 400+ hours of class work, labs, projects and even a couple hackathons! not only did I learn about JavaScript, React, web development best practices, and RESTful architecture- I also learned about the value of collaboration, team work, how necessary a skill time management really is... I came out of the experience with a whole new toolbelt with which to tackle my goals.

My academic career was spent at the University of Washington in Seattle where I studied philosophy and read the classic Greek greats, found a fondness for Hume, had my foundations stripped by Descartes and discovered a curiosity for the sublime in Schopenhauer.

I sought answers to the questions about the nature of reality and personal purpose and came out with just more questions- big success!

Throughout university and thereafter I continued to work as a professional fashion model and have travelled around the U.S. strutting down catwalks and shooting for online catalogues.

Currently I'm represented by TCM Models in Seattle, State MGMT in Los Angeles, and major models in Milano, italy.

I've been around tech my whole life with a father who worked for Nintendo, Taito, & Microsoft and a brother who studied 3D animation and went on to become a qa lead at Big Fish Games.

My mother, being on the other side as a creative/maker, inspired my involvement in practicing art. These influences together have given me a lense that helped me fully appreciate the wonder and potential computers could offer.

I consider myself a human of renaissance ideals and as such my telos is geared towards fostering personal development and becoming a productive citizen of earth. I am especially interested in contributing to projects involving education technology, environmental sustainability, and art!

The TL;DR: basically I'm a pretty cool human who loves to breakdance, paint watercolor landscapes, go on long walks, read eastern philosophy(or anything fantasy/sci-fi), and it so happens that I'm also a web developer!
